## 1. COLOR PALETTE (Hex Codes)

We use a "Medical + Vitality" palette. It avoids the cold "hospital white" look by adding warm accents.

## 2. TYPOGRAPHY (Fonts)

Clean, sans-serif, modern. Legibility is priority #1.

- **Primary Font (Headings):** `Montserrat` (Bold / ExtraBold).
  - _Usage:_ Headlines, Logo Text, Big numbers.
- **Secondary Font (Body):** `Open Sans` or `Roboto` (Regular / Medium).
  - _Usage:_ Paragraphs, lists, details.

---

## 3. VISUAL STYLE

### Photography (Flyers & Social Media)

- **Style:** Realistic, bright, natural light.
- **Subjects:** Real people showing _relief_ or _movement_ (not just sitting).
- **Avoid:** Generic 3D cartoons, overly fake "stock photo smiles", dark environments.
- **Key Composition:** Always leave "Negative Space" (clean background area) for text overlay.

### Illustration (Icons & Diagrams)

- **Style:** Minimalist line art or flat vector.
- **Stroke:** Medium weight, rounded corners.
- **Color:** Deep Blue or Green.

---

## 4. IMAGE GENERATION RULES (For DALL-E/Midjourney)

When generating images, ALWAYS include these keywords to maintain consistency:

> "Professional medical photography, bright natural lighting, modern minimalist clinic aesthetic, teal and deep blue color palette, high resolution, soft bokeh background."

**Negative Prompts (What to avoid):**

> "Cartoon, illustration, 3D render, dark shadows, text inside image, distorted hands, messy medical equipment, cluttered background."
